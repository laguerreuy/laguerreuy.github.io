We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, to comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations, and to resolve disputes. You may request deletion as described below.

8. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, Firebase Security Rules restricting database access, and authenticated access for staff. No system is completely secure, but we work to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
