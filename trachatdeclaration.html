Child Safety Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Our application is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users. We enforce a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Scope

These standards apply to all content and interactions within our app, including user profiles, messages, media uploads, comments, groups, and any other features or services.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Creation, possession, sharing, or distribution of child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Any sexual content involving minors, real or fictional.
  • Grooming, solicitation, or manipulation of minors for sexual purposes.
  • Requests for sexual images or information from minors.
  • Links or references to content that exploits or sexualizes minors.

3. Detection and Prevention

  • Automated and manual moderation of user-generated content.
  • User reporting tools available within the app.
  • Immediate review of reported content and accounts.

4. Enforcement Actions

Any violation of these standards may result in:

  • Immediate removal of content.
  • Suspension or termination of user accounts.
  • Reporting to relevant authorities and child protection organizations.

5. Reporting CSAE

If you encounter content or behavior that may involve CSAE, please report it immediately:

  • In-app reporting feature
  • Email: info@softlogic.app

6. Legal Compliance

We cooperate fully with law enforcement and comply with all applicable child protection laws and regulations.

7. Contact

For questions regarding these standards, contact us at:
